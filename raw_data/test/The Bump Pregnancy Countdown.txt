The Bump Privacy Policy
Updated: January 4, 2023


At The Bump, the privacy of our users is of utmost importance to us. We recognize that pregnancy is a highly personal life event, and we take extra precautions in ensuring our users' data is protected. We understand that privacy matters, and we limit the amount of personal information we collect to what is necessary to provide our users the experience they expect on The Bump.


This Privacy Policy describes the data protection practices of The Bump (“we,” “us,” or “our”).

This Privacy Policy applies to the website and mobile application operated by The Bump.

We obtain information about you through the means discussed below when you use our services. The information we collect and the purposes for which we use it will depend to some extent on the specific services you use and how you interact with The Bump. The following section describes the categories of information about you that we collect and how we collect and use such information. See the following section for information regarding the purposes for which we collect information.

Your choices and rights

No matter where you reside, on The Bump you have certain rights with respect to your information as further described in this Section, in addition to any rights discussed elsewhere in this Privacy Policy. The Bump will honor the following rights:


Update or correct your information; you can at any time contact The Bump to change the information you have provided to us.
Delete your information; you can request us to delete information related to you within our systems. Please be aware to honor this right The Bump will fully anonymize your information.
Opt-out of marketing communications; you can let us know that you do not want to receive any marketing communications from us. You still will receive transactional communications.

We will consider all requests and provide our response within the time period stated by applicable law. Please note, however, that certain information may be exempt from such requests in some circumstances, which may include if we need to keep processing your information for our legitimate interests or to comply with a legal obligation. We will let you know where this is the case or if certain rights don’t apply in your country or state of residence. We may request that you provide us with information necessary to verify your identity before responding to your request as required or permitted by applicable law. If you are a California resident, please see the “Privacy Information for California Residents” section on the footer of our website for information about your specific rights under California law.


You can exercise any of the rights mentioned above directing your request to the contact information at the end of this privacy policy. For a deletion request, you can also use this form.

Children’s privacy

The services are intended for general audiences and not for children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected “personal information” (as defined by the United States Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) from children under the age of 13 without legally-valid parental consent, we will take reasonable steps to anonymize it as soon as possible. We also comply with other age restrictions and requirements in accordance with applicable local laws.


How we protect your information

We take a variety of industry standard security measures (such as encryption in transit and at rest) to protect your information against accidental or unlawful destruction or accidental loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or access. However, no method of transmission, and no means of electronic or physical storage, is absolutely secure. As such, you acknowledge and accept that we cannot guarantee the security of your information transmitted to, through, or on our services or via the Internet and that any such transmission is at your own risk.


When you sign up for an account, you will be required to establish a username and password. If you create an account with us, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password and for any activity that occurs under your account. We are not responsible for any loss or damage arising from your failure to maintain the confidentiality of your password.


Retention of your information

We will keep your personal information for as long as is necessary to provide you the services and as long as you are an active user of our platform. Thereafter, we will keep your personal information for as long as is necessary:


To respond to any questions, complaints, or claims made by you or on your behalf;
To show that we treated you fairly; or
To keep records required by law.

We will not retain your personal information for longer than necessary for the purposes set out in this policy. Different retention periods apply for different types of personal information, and you have the right to delete certain categories of your information.


Changes to our privacy policy
