Privacy Policy for Baby Tracker
This privacy policy governs your use of Baby Tracker that was created by Nighp Software LLC ("Nighp")

Baby Tracker will not obtain your personal information if you do not use sync feature of Baby Tracker.
Baby Tracker may obtain certain personal information you provide when you use sync feature with Baby Tracker Server. Such information includes but not limited to the following: (a) your baby's name; (b) your email address; (c) your baby's date of birth; (d) your baby's growth data; (e) all records that you input in Baby Tracker; (f) all photos that you input in Baby Tracker.
If you use sync feature with iCloud or Dropbox, Baby Tracker will not obtain your personal information if you use sync feature with iCloud or Dropbox, but iCloud or Dropbox may collect your personal information. Please check the privacy policies of iCloud or Dropbox before you use sync feature with iCloud or Dropbox.
We may use the information you provide only for syncing data between your devices. We will not disclose such information to any third parties.
We will retain your data for as long as you use Baby Tracker and for a reasonable time thereafter.
Please be aware that, although we endeavor provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
By using the sync feature with Baby Tracker Server, you are consenting to our collecting and using of the information you provide as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.
If you have any questions regarding privacy while using Baby Tracker, or if you would like to request that your account and associated data be deleted, or if you have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at babytracker@nighp.com.
This version of the privacy policy is effective as of 7/4/2015.
